Owncloud 7 - upgrade from 6

OwnCloud 7 is here for more than three months (changelog). How to upgrade from OwnCloud 6?

The good news: it's easy.

  • Make backups
  • Download .tar.gz package and extract it
  • Copy owncloud6/config/config.php to owncloud7/config/config.pjp
  • Run migration tasks by: sudo -u apache php occ upgrade
